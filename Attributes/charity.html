CHARITY

Day 1

What Prophets have said about charity

Watch this talk from President Monson on charity & love. As you watch, reflect on the following questions:

  1. How would being more charitable change my relationships with others?
  2. How would being more charitable change the way I see myself?
  3. How do I develop charity?

Day 2

What do the scriptures say about charity?

Read the following scriptures about charity. As you read, ponder the same questions you pondered yesterday while watching the talk:

  1. How would being more charitable change my relationships with others?
  2. How would being more charitable change the way I see myself?
  3. How do I develop charity?

Day 3

What was the author trying to say in these scriptures?

It is common to read scriptures from the perspective of your own culture and life experiences. That is a great way to apply the scriptures to your life, but it is equally important to understand what the author was really trying to say from the perspective of their culture and life experiences. Visit the following website to understand the scriptures in their original language. This can help uncover the true meaning of what the author was trying to say.

Day 4

What are your experiences with charity?

Reflect on experiences in your life when you witnessed an act of charity. Describe that experience in detail. How did you feel? Why was that experience memorable for you?

After writing out your story, make a plan to create another experience like that and do it!
